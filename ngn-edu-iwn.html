Objectives of Nigeria Google for Education Program:
  1. The Program is to expand the use of Google Workspace for Education Technology & Tools purposely built for digital learning and innovations
  2. To help Schools (K-12) and Higher Education to achieve Hybrid/Blended learning environment which is standard for educational institutions after covid-19 pandemic
Benefits of the Nigeria Google for Education Program:
  1. Upgrade of Secured Internet Infrastructure to all Classroom, Hostels, Offices and the entire campus to 21st century technology (WI-FI 6)
  2. Deployment of Chromebook Laptops for all Students and academic staff.
  3. Onboarding, Training and Capacity Development for the Educators and Staffs for effective use of Google Workspace for delivering learning and innovation for the students
  4. Google Workspace Educational Plus account for Students and Staffs
  5. Up to 4Gbps/4Gbps Internet backbone directly from Google Equiano Submarine Cable
  6. Infrastructure fully powered by renewable energy for Google zero carbon footprint
